About Me

I am an aspiring PhD candidate in Biotechnology at Peking University, where I am honored to be co-advised by Prof. Deng Xingwang and Research Fellow He Hang. Prior to joining Peking University, I will receive dual Bachelor's degrees in Agronomy and Engineering from Southwest University in June 2026. My academic journey has been shaped by the Shennong Class (Honors Program) at Honghong College, which laid a strong interdisciplinary foundation in life sciences and computer science. My research focuses on AI and computational biology, with a particular emphasis on large models for smart breeding. Explore my publications and projects below, or see my CV. I also maintain personal blogs. Feel free to reach out via email.
